ESA - the European Space Agency - was formed in 1975 and represents its member states in anything space-related.


ESA astronauts Thomas Pesquet and Mathias Maurer will launch to the International Space Station in 2021.


They have a range of opportunities for students, school pupils and professionals. Find out more here:


  • Kids (ages 5 - 12)
  • University Students
  • Recent Graduates
  • Professionals
  • Teachers

For students there are many opportunities through ESA Academy. These are split into Training and Learning Programmes (like the one where we met!) and Hands-On Programmes (like Spin Your Thesis!). You can hear about all their future opportunities by signing up to the mailing list.
