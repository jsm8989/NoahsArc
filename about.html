About

Team

Our 4 team members met at a ESA summer school in 2020. Fast forward 6 months and they found out they'd been selected to take part in scientific research using ESA's facilities! Here they are:

Shireen

Our team leader Shireen is a Master’s student in aerospace engineering at the Georgia Institute of Technology in the USA. When asked what she would smuggle onto the ISS (given the opportunity) she said “a portable translator” – to talk to any alien visitors!

Giuliana

Having recently graduated in Physics and Philosophy from the University of Nottingham, Giuliana is our only non-student team member. Her current Technology Analyst role in the financial technology industry leads well to her Project Management role for the team!

Katie

Our resident biologist Katie is studying biochemistry at the University of Warwick. She has also been the driving force behind our outreach and is the one sorting out our social media!

Jamie

Jamie, one of our talented scientific officers, is currently studying Physics at the University of Cambridge. He will mostly be working on the experimental design and analysis of our project results!
