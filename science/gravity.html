Gravity

The strange force of nature which affects humans in so many ways

You may have heard of the currently accepted view of gravity, known as the "General Theory of Relativity". Unfortunately, we can't quite explain all the details of that on this webpage; we will instead highlight a little bit about how it's relevant to our project and how we simulate different gravitational conditions on Earth.
